My Story

My Story

"Great design isn't just how it looks — it's how it works, how it feels, and who it serves."

I grew up sketching logos in the margins of school notebooks — way before I knew what a design system was. At seventeen I taught myself HTML from a library book, and something clicked: here was a medium where visual thinking and logical precision could coexist beautifully.

After studying Interaction Design at university, I spent three years inside a fast-paced product agency, shipping work for startups and Fortune 500 companies alike. In 2019, I took the leap to freelance — and never looked back.

Today I work with founders, creative directors, and growth teams who want their digital presence to be felt, not just seen. My process is collaborative, my timelines are tight, and my standards are non-negotiable.

When I'm not in Figma or a code editor, you'll find me in a specialty coffee shop, hunting for obscure typefaces, or hiking somewhere with no phone signal.
